About

Hands-on engineer in the making

I'm a mechanical engineering student who learns best by building things and then figuring out why they behave the way they do. My coursework spans CAD design in Siemens NX and Inventor through to simulation in ANSYS and MATLAB/Simulink — including a finite-element model I validated against a physical test and a multibody-dynamics simulation I built and wrote up on my own.

Alongside my studies I've completed two R&D internships and funded much of my education through hands-on construction and renovation work, which taught me precision, reliability, and how to keep going when a problem is hard. In autumn 2026 I'm heading to the University of Twente for an exchange in AI in Engineering and Biorobotics.
